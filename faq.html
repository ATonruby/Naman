FAQs

  1. What is NAMAN?

    NAMAN stands for Navi Mumbai Angel Network, we provide a platform for seed and early stage investing. The company acts as a facilitator and undertakes function such as Due Diligence, Business Valuations, Business Modeling, et cetra on behalf of NAMAN members.

  2. Who can become the members?

    The members are whoever, who would like to invest in early stage companies and also mentor them with their expertise and networks.

  3. When do the investors meet?

    Members are informed about the investors meet via mails.

  4. Does NAMAN or its members sign any NDA before receiving company information?

    No, it is not possible to sign NDAs with each startup. Entrepreneurs are advised to share the information which may not impact their business.

  1. Are there guidelines for presentations and business plans?

    Yes, there are guidelines for presentations. Please ask the NAMAN team to send you the guidelines once you have registered ZoomStarters Club (ZSC)

  2. What is the valuation to expect for our company?

    Please share your valuation expectations with the team before your presentation. As valuations are always subjective, a forensic valuation might help you for a broader perspective. Please contact your portfolio representative for further perusal.

  3. What is the maximum money I can raise?

    Generally, the members together can support around 5 to 50 lakhs. However, in association with other angel networks this figure could go upto 100 lakhs timeframes for companies raising more that 50 lakhs will be higher.

  1. What is the timeframe for investment?

    Usually it takes up to 2-8 months to invest, from the date of the first presentation.

  2. Do we need to engage a lawyer?

    It is completely up to the company.

  3. Any other expectations from the companies

    We expect companies shortlisted for presentation to provide information to the best of their knowledge. If the forensic due diligence contradicts with the information provided, the investment process is likely to be cancelled. Any change in the company that occurs during the process of the investment, please inform your portfolio representative.

  4. What is the support Lead Angels provides post investment?

    Pre investment support is completely provided by ZSC team. Post investment NAMAN and ZSC teams works with the startup to assist it key areas like future capital raise, business strategy, business connections, networking, PR, and talent search.
